Last Chance Animal Shelter

In a world where you only get one shot, one opportunity, we are making sure these pets don't take it for granted. Or sometimes at all.

Meet FIFO - we only have room for two animals so her name is our rule - one way or another. She's exceptionally sweet but maybe too clingy? It's kind of weird no one has adopted her yet. Oh well. Guess we shouldn't get our hopes up.
This is Not Yet - something we hope they won't have to say for long! They got their name after their third loss in the underground fighting ring we voluntell our residents to participate in. Maybe she's the right choice for you! After all, she still is wearing her mask despite being vaccinated. That's more consideration than her opponents showed her in the ring!
